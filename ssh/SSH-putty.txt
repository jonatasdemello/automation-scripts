Click "Auth" under "SSH."

Deselect the
	"Bypass Authentication Entirely,"
	"Attempt Authentication Using Pageant" and
	"Attempt TLS or CryptoCard Auth"
check boxes by clicking them if they're enabled.

Verify the "Attempt Keyboard-Interactive Auth" check box is enabled. Click it if it isn't enabled.
